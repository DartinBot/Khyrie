Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the 7-day free trial?
Full access to all premium features of your chosen plan, including AI workout generation, advanced analytics, and priority support. Cancel anytime during the trial period at no charge.
Can I change or cancel my subscription?
Yes! You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle, and you'll retain access until your current period ends.
How does AI workout generation work?
Our AI analyzes your fitness level, goals, available time, and equipment to create personalized workouts. It learns from your feedback and adapts to ensure optimal results and progression.
Is family sharing really unlimited?
Pro and Elite plans include unlimited family members. Each family member gets their own personalized experience while sharing progress and motivation within your family group.
What devices are supported?
Khyrie works on all modern devices - smartphones, tablets, laptops, and desktops. Our Progressive Web App provides a native app experience on mobile devices.
